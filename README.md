Design and Development of a Frontend Website for FrostFresh Cold Store
Problem Statement
Many local cold store businesses lack a professional online presence to display their products and services. Customers often do not have access to important information such as available frozen products, prices, store location, contact details, and operating hours before visiting the store. This limits customer convenience and reduces effective engagement between the business and its customers.
Target Audience
•	Customers seeking frozen food products such as meat, fish, chicken, and other frozen items
•	Walk-in customers who want to preview products before visiting the store
•	New customers searching online for reliable local cold store services
Core Features
•	Home page with business overview and highlights
•	Product listing with categories, images, prices, and brief descriptions
•	About Us page describing the cold store business
•	Contact page with phone number, email, location, and working hours
•	Responsive design for mobile and desktop devices
•	Clean and user-friendly interface
